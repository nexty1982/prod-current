# OMAI Installation Guide for Samsung Galaxy Z Fold 6

This guide provides step-by-step instructions for installing the OMAI (Orthodox Metrics AI) application on your Samsung Galaxy Z Fold 6 device.

## Device Compatibility

The OMAI application is compatible with the Samsung Galaxy Z Fold 6, which runs Android 14. The application requires:
- Android 7.0 (Nougat) or higher (minimum SDK 24)
- Internet connectivity for online features
- Microphone access for voice interaction

## Installation Methods

There are three ways to install the OMAI application on your Galaxy Z Fold 6:

### Method 1: Direct APK Installation (For Internal Testing)

This method is recommended for internal testers who have received the APK file directly.

#### Prerequisites:
- The OMAI APK file (either `latest-debug.apk` or `omai-release.apk`)
- USB cable or alternative method to transfer the APK to your device

#### Installation Steps:

1. **Enable Installation from Unknown Sources**:
   - Open the **Settings** app on your Galaxy Z Fold 6
   - Navigate to **Security and privacy**
   - Tap on **Install unknown apps** or **Unknown sources**
   - Select the app you'll use to install the APK (e.g., Files, Chrome, etc.)
   - Toggle the switch to allow installations from this source

2. **Transfer the APK to Your Device**:
   - Connect your Galaxy Z Fold 6 to your computer using a USB cable
   - Select "File Transfer" mode on your phone when prompted
   - Copy the APK file to your device's storage
   - Alternatively, you can download the APK directly to your device if you received it via email or messaging app

3. **Install the APK**:
   - Open the **Files** app (or your preferred file manager) on your Galaxy Z Fold 6
   - Navigate to the location where you saved the APK file
   - Tap on the APK file to start the installation process
   - Review the permissions and tap **Install**
   - Wait for the installation to complete and tap **Open** to launch the app

### Method 2: Firebase App Distribution (For Invited Testers)

This method is for testers who have been invited to test the app through Firebase App Distribution.

#### Prerequisites:
- An invitation email from the OMAI development team
- A Google account

#### Installation Steps:

1. **Accept the Invitation**:
   - Open the invitation email on your Galaxy Z Fold 6
   - Tap the invitation link in the email
   - Sign in with your Google account if prompted

2. **Install Firebase App Tester**:
   - If you don't already have it, you'll be prompted to install the Firebase App Tester app from the Google Play Store
   - Follow the on-screen instructions to install it

3. **Download and Install OMAI**:
   - After installing Firebase App Tester, you'll see the OMAI app available for download
   - Tap **Download** to get the app
   - Once downloaded, tap **Install** to install the app on your device
   - After installation completes, you can open the app directly

### Method 3: Google Play Store (For Internal Test Track)

This method is for testers who have been invited to the internal test track on Google Play.

#### Prerequisites:
- An invitation email from the OMAI development team
- A Google account associated with the test program

#### Installation Steps:

1. **Accept the Invitation**:
   - Open the invitation email on your Galaxy Z Fold 6
   - Tap the invitation link in the email
   - Sign in with your Google account if prompted

2. **Join the Testing Program**:
   - Follow the on-screen instructions to join the testing program
   - Tap **Become a tester** when prompted

3. **Install from Google Play**:
   - After joining the program, you'll be directed to the OMAI app page on Google Play
   - Tap **Install** to download and install the app
   - Once installation is complete, you can open the app from Google Play or your app drawer

## Post-Installation Setup

After installing the OMAI app on your Galaxy Z Fold 6, follow these steps to set up the application:

1. **Launch the App**:
   - Open the OMAI app from your app drawer

2. **Login**:
   - Enter your Church ID
   - Enter your User ID
   - Enter your Password
   - Tap **Login**

3. **Grant Permissions**:
   - When prompted, grant the necessary permissions for the app to function properly:
     - **Internet access**: Required for connecting to the OMAI service
     - **Microphone access**: Required for voice interaction features

4. **Configure Settings** (Optional):
   - Navigate to the Settings screen to configure language preferences and other options
   - You can access this by tapping the menu icon and selecting "Settings"

## Optimizing for Galaxy Z Fold 6

The OMAI application uses Jetpack Compose for its user interface, which should adapt well to the Galaxy Z Fold 6's unique form factor. Here are some tips for optimal use:

1. **Utilize the Large Screen**:
   - When unfolded, the app will take advantage of the larger screen to display more content
   - The conversation interface and memory management screens benefit from the additional screen real estate

2. **Flex Mode**:
   - You can partially fold your device to use it in Flex mode for certain activities
   - This can be useful during voice interactions, allowing you to set the phone down while speaking

3. **Multi-Window Support**:
   - You can use OMAI alongside other apps in split-screen or pop-up view
   - This is helpful for referencing information while conversing with the assistant

## Troubleshooting

If you encounter issues during installation or use of the OMAI app on your Galaxy Z Fold 6, try these troubleshooting steps:

1. **Installation Fails**:
   - Ensure you have sufficient storage space on your device
   - Check that you've enabled installation from unknown sources (for direct APK installation)
   - Verify that you're using the correct APK for your device

2. **App Crashes or Performs Poorly**:
   - Ensure your device is running the latest Android version
   - Close background apps to free up memory
   - Restart your device and try again

3. **Login Issues**:
   - Verify your internet connection
   - Double-check your Church ID, User ID, and Password
   - Contact your administrator if you continue to experience issues

4. **Voice Recognition Problems**:
   - Ensure you've granted microphone permissions to the app
   - Check that no other apps are using the microphone
   - Speak clearly and in a quiet environment
